  • Responsibilities

    1. ENSURING MAXIMUM PARTICIPATION IN APOGEE 2018

      The primary task of every Campus Ambassador is to ensure maximum participation in APOGEE 2018 from their college. The efforts of the Campus Ambassador in this direction will be judged by a referral code system, with each Campus Ambassadors referral code being his/her name as mentioned during registration. This has to be entered by the participant in the field referred by during registration.

    2. SOCIAL MEDIA PUBLICITY
      1. FACEBOOK
        1. Sharing of all posts of APOGEE 2018 via your Facebook Account.
        2. Sharing Facebook Stories about APOGEE 2018.
      2. INSTAGRAM
        1. Sharing of all posts of APOGEE 2018 via your Instagram Account.
        2. Sharing Instagram Stories about APOGEE 2018
      3. WHATSAPP
        1. Sending messages to Whatsapp contacts about APOGEE 2018
        2. Sharing Whatsapp Stories about APOGEE 2018
      4. NOTE
        • All posts will be monitored and updated weekly after viewing your social media handles.
        • All stories will be monitored by your point of contact from BITS Pilani after you send them a screenshot that clearly mentions the story being updated at least 10 hours ago.
    3. PUTTING UP PHYSICAL POSTERS

      APOGEE will be sending the Campus Ambassadors posters via post to be put up physically in the campus of your college. This will be verified via photos sent by the campus ambassador to their point of contact from BITS Pilani. Only those posters that are sent by APOGEE for this purpose will be counted here.

    4. SENDING ROUTE MAILS

      APOGEE will be sending the Campus Ambassadors emails that have to be circulated throughout the college via a route mail system or mass mailing. Points for this will be awarded only when the campus ambassador cc’s their point of contact from BITS Pilani in mail so that they have access to the recipient list. Only those emails that are sent by APOGEE for this purpose will be counted here.

    5. ORGANIZING WORKSHOPS

      Any campus ambassador interested in organizing a workshop in their college should contact us regarding the same at least 10 days prior to the date of the workshop. We will connect you with a workshop organizing team of our choice in your city to conduct the workshop, and you will be responsible for ensuring that all logistics are in place and maximum students attend the workshop. The workshop must have a minimum participation of 10 students. The topics for the workshop are as follows:

      EE/EC/EI Mechanical/Civil CS/IT
      PLC Autodesk autocad Java
      Scada Catia .NET
      Ecad Solidworks PHP
      Robotics Ansys Web Design
      IOT Creo Big data
      VLSI Stadd Pro Cloud Computing
      Matlab 3ds max Amazon web services
      Revit Blockchain
      3D printer IOT
      Python
      Machine learning
      Android
      iOS
      Sas
      SAP

      The organizer of the workshop that has the maximum student attendance, couple with feedback from the Workshop Organizing Team selected by APOGEE, will be rewarded with a guaranteed internship with our partners. It is suggested that the Campus Ambassador takes a survey in their college about the preferred topic for the workshop to ensure maximum engagement in the workshop.

    6. ORGANIZING APOGEE INFORMATIVE SESSIONS

      Campus Ambassadors will have to organize an APOGEE Informative Session in their college. The information to be given will be sent across by the APOGEE team, and the attendees must include all the heads of clubs and technical societies in the college. This task must be done anytime between 28th January and 11 February only. This will be monitored via a 5 minute video of the session which must be sent by the Campus Ambassador to the point of contact from BITS Pilani. Moreover, the session must have a minimum attendance of 30 people and the Campus Ambassador must send a photo of a sheet containing the Names, Contacts Numbers, Email IDs and Designations of the attendees which they can ask the attendees to fill when they come for the session.

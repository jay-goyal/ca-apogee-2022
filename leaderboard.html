  • Responsibilities

    1. ENSURING MAXIMUM PARTICIPATION IN APOGEE 2022

      The primary task of every Campus Ambassador is to ensure maximum participation from their college. The efforts of the Campus Ambassador in this direction will be judged by a referral code system, with each Campus Ambassadors referral code being his/her name as mentioned during registration.

      Points Allocation for Registration: 100 points per participant.Until APOGEE, this will be monitored on the basis of number of registrations on the website. However, after APOGEE, the points from this will be revised based on the number of students who attended APOGEE.

      We strongly advise you to ensure that your referral code is advertised well, and everyone is requested to mention your name in the referred by field in the registration form, otherwise your efforts will not be translated into points on the leaderboard.

    2. SOCIAL MEDIA PUBLICITY
      1. FACEBOOK

        1. Sharing of all posts of APOGEE 2022 via your Facebook Account.

          Points Allocation: Each post = 50 points

          Each Share =20 points

          Both these things will be monitored and updated weekly after viewing your timeline.

        2. Sharing Facebook Stories about APOGEE 2022

          Points Allocation: 1 story = 10 points

          This will be monitored by your point of contact from BITS

          Pilani after you send them a screenshot that mentions the story being updated at least 10 hours ago.

      2. INSTAGRAM

        1. Sharing Instagram Stories about APOGEE 2022

          Points Allocation: 1 story = 50 points (see table at the end of the document for another metric for points)

          This will be monitored by your point of contact from BITS Pilani after you send them a screenshot that mentions the story being updated at least 10 hours ago.

      3. WHATSAPP
        1. Sending messages to Whatsapp contacts about APOGEE 2022

        2. Sharing WhatsApp Stories about APOGEE 2022 Points Allocation: 1 story = 30 points

          (see table at the end of the document for another metric for points)

    3. PUTTING UP PHYSICAL POSTERS

      (Points allocation for this vertical will be on a college basis, this task does not in any way condemn the CA to disregard the social distancing norms being followed. If college of the CA opens, he/she may contact anyone from BITS Pilani for further perusal into the matter)

      APOGEE will be sending the Campus Ambassadors posters via post to be put up physically in the campus of your college.

      Points Allocation: 500 points

      This will be verified via photos sent by the campus ambassador to their point of contact from BITS Pilani. Please note that only those posters that are sent by APOGEE for this purpose will be counted here.

    4. SENDING ROUTE MAILS

      APOGEE will be sending the Campus Ambassadors emails that have to be circulated throughout the college via a route mail system or mass mailing.

      Points Allocation: 500 points per route email

      This will be verified via screenshots sent by the campus ambassador to their point of contact from BITS Pilani. Please note that only those emails that are sent by APOGEE for this purpose will be counted here.

    5. ORGANIZING WORKSHOPS

      Any campus ambassador interested in organizing a workshop in their college can contact us regarding the same. APOGEE will help you in organizing the same, and you will be responsible for ensuring that all logistics are in place and maximum students attend the workshop.

      Points for workshop or manifest: 4042 points

      Organizing workshop has various incentives as mentioned in the next part of the document, in addition there are direct cash incentives for the organizing committee, depending on the turnout for the workshop.

      The organizer of the workshop that has the maximum student attendance, couple with feedback from the Workshop Organizing Team selected by APOGEE, will be rewarded with a guaranteed internship with our partners.

    6. ORGANIZING APOGEE INFORMATIVE SESSIONS

      Campus Ambassadors will have to organize an APOGEE Informative Session in their college. The information to be given will be sent across by the APOGEE team, and the attendees must include all the heads of clubs and technical societies in the college.

      Points Allocation: 200 per session (minimum 40 attendance)

      This will be monitored via pictures of the session as sent to the point of contact from BITS Pilani, along with a presence of an APOGEE team member at the session. The details of the same will be conveyed in due time.
